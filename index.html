Here's a simple and quick chicken curry recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder or paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk or water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  • Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  • Stir in curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for another minute.
  • Add pureed tomatoes and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala (spice mix).
  • Add chicken pieces, stirring to coat with the masala. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • Add coconut milk or water, mix, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or bread.

Enjoy your chicken curry!
